# NewsGen AI - Project One-Pager

## Overview
An AI-powered platform that transforms news headlines into publication-ready articles with a single click, reducing content creation time from hours to seconds.

---

## Value Proposition
**"From headline to article in one click"**

NewsGen AI solves the critical bottleneck in content creation by automating article writing while maintaining quality, enabling content teams to publish 10x more content with the same resources.

---

## Target Customers

**Primary:** Content Marketing Managers at tech companies (10-500 employees)
- Need: 5-10 articles/week minimum
- Pain: Limited writing resources, tight deadlines
- Value: 100x faster content production

**Secondary:** Freelance creators, digital publishers, social media agencies, corporate communications

**Market Size:** 4M+ content marketing professionals globally, growing 15% YoY

---

## Key Features

1. **Real-time news search** across thousands of sources
2. **One-click AI generation** (10-15 seconds per article)
3. **Article management** with gallery/list views and full-screen reader
4. **Search history** with autocomplete for workflow efficiency
5. **Professional UX** with dark mode and mobile-responsive design

---

## Technology

- **Frontend:** React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Architecture:** Modern component-based with custom hooks
- **Integration:** AI API via webhooks
- **Status:** Production-ready, Supabase integration ready
